# 🚗 Austo Automobiles Customer Analysis

## 📌 Business Problem

Austo Motor Company is facing challenges in optimizing its marketing campaigns. The objective of this project is to analyze customer data to understand purchasing behavior and improve targeting strategies.

---

## 🎯 Objectives

* Analyze gender-based preferences for different car types
* Evaluate the likelihood of salaried individuals purchasing specific car segments
* Understand spending behavior across customer segments
* Examine the impact of loans and income on purchasing decisions
* Identify factors influencing high-value automobile purchases

---

## 📊 Dataset Overview

The dataset consists of **1,581 observations and 14 variables**, including:

* Demographics: Age, Gender, Marital Status
* Financials: Salary, Partner Salary, Total Salary
* Purchase Data: Car Make, Price
* Loan Information: Personal & House Loans

---

## 🛠 Tools & Technologies

* Python (Pandas, NumPy)
* Data Visualization (Matplotlib, Seaborn)
* Jupyter Notebook

---

## 🔍 Approach

### 1. Data Cleaning

* Handled missing values using imputation
* Corrected inconsistent categorical values
* Derived new features such as Age Groups
* Calculated missing partner salaries

### 2. Exploratory Data Analysis

* Univariate analysis (distribution of salary, gender, etc.)
* Bivariate analysis (relationships between variables)
* Correlation analysis to identify key drivers

---

## 💡 Key Insights

* Female customers tend to spend more on automobiles compared to males
* Higher income groups prefer premium cars such as SUVs
* Partner salary significantly influences total purchasing capacity
* Salaried individuals show strong interest in Sedan cars
* Age positively correlates with car price (older customers buy higher-priced vehicles)

---

## 📈 Business Recommendations

* Target high-income and dual-income households for premium vehicle campaigns
* Focus SUV marketing on female and high-spending segments
* Design tailored financing options for customers with loans
* Improve segmentation strategy based on income and demographics
* Optimize campaigns by focusing on high-conversion customer profiles
